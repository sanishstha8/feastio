How it works

Up and running in minutes

1

Manager logs in

The manager signs in to the admin portal, sets up the menu categories and items, and adds the team.

2

Staff log in to their portal

Waiters and kitchen staff log in with their accounts and land directly on their role-specific view.

3

Waiter takes an order

The waiter selects a table, picks items from the menu and places the order. It instantly appears in the kitchen.

4

Kitchen prepares and marks ready

Kitchen staff update each item from pending → preparing → ready. The waiter can see the status live.

5

Order complete, table freed

When the order is completed the table status automatically resets to available — ready for the next guest.
